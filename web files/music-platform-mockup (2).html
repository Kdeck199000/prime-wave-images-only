About PrimeWave

Revolutionizing Music Distribution in Lesotho

PrimeWave was born from a simple belief: artists deserve to earn fairly from their creative work. While streaming platforms pay fractions of cents per play, we ensure artists keep 67% of every download - putting real money back into the pockets of Lesotho's incredible musical talent.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where Basotho artists can thrive financially from their music while fans get direct access to high-quality, original content. We bridge the gap between artistic passion and financial success in the Kingdom of Lesotho.
